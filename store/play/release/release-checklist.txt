Google Play release checklist

App identity:

- Package name: net.chargeledger
- App name: Charge Ledger
- Category: Tools or Auto & Vehicles.
- Contact email: support@chargeledger.net
- Privacy policy URL: https://chargeledger.net/privacy.html

Before uploading:

- Confirm Android app signing is configured.
- Confirm android/key.properties exists locally with the upload key values.
- Confirm versionCode has increased.
- Confirm versionName is user-facing and correct.
- Generate a release AAB.
- Upload to internal testing first.
- Check Play pre-launch report.

Store listing:

- App name entered from store/shared/listing/en-US/title.txt.
- Short description entered from store/shared/listing/en-US/short-description.txt.
- Full description entered from store/shared/listing/en-US/full-description.txt.
- App icon uploaded from store/shared/assets/app-icon.png.
- Screenshots uploaded.
- Feature graphic uploaded from store/shared/assets/feature-graphic.png.
- Internal tester Google Groups configured from store/play/play-config.json appDetails.testerGoogleGroups.

App content:

- Privacy policy URL added: https://chargeledger.net/privacy.html.
- Data Safety form completed.
- App access instructions completed with demo login.
- Content rating questionnaire completed.
- Target audience and content completed.
- Ads declaration completed.
- News app declaration completed.
- Government app declaration completed.
- Financial features declaration completed if shown.

After internal testing:

- Verify install and login flow on a real Android device.
- Verify Zaptec sync.
- Verify filters, totals and PDF export.
- Verify logout and local data behavior.
