Used by App Store:

- Reuse the shared screenshots and graphics when the dimensions satisfy App
  Store Connect requirements.
- App Store-specific iPhone screenshots currently live under
  store/app_store/assets/iphone-screenshots because the shared Google Play phone
  screenshots do not match the App Store phone screenshot sizes.
