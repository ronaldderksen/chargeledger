App Store privacy notes

Use these notes when completing App Store Connect privacy details. Review them
against the exact uploaded iOS build before submission.

Data handling:

- Zaptec email/account name is used for authentication and app functionality.
- Zaptec password is sent to Zaptec over HTTPS for authentication and is not
  stored by Charge Ledger.
- Charger and charge session data is synchronized from Zaptec and stored locally
  in the native app for app functionality.
- App preferences such as filters, visible columns, kWh price, and currency are
  stored for app functionality.
- Firebase Analytics logs privacy-focused app interaction events such as login
  result, demo login, sync result, filter category changes, and PDF export.

Analytics event payloads should avoid account identifiers, charger names, exact
dates, energy values, and cost values.

Review before submission:

- Confirm the uploaded iOS build's Firebase Analytics configuration.
- Confirm whether Apple requires device identifiers or usage data disclosures
  for the Firebase Analytics configuration used in the build.
- Confirm there are no advertising, crash reporting, or extra data-collecting
  SDKs beyond Firebase Analytics.
