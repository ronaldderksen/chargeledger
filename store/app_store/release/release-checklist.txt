App Store release checklist

App identity:

- Bundle ID: net.chargeledger
- App name: Charge Ledger
- Primary language: English (U.S.)
- Support URL: https://chargeledger.net/
- Privacy policy URL: https://chargeledger.net/privacy.html

Shared content:

- Review listing copy in store/shared/listing/en-US.
- Review release notes in store/shared/release/release-notes-internal.txt.
- Review screenshots and graphics in store/shared/assets.
- Review App Store-specific iPhone screenshots in
  store/app_store/assets/iphone-screenshots.

Before uploading:

- Confirm Apple signing and provisioning are configured.
- Confirm the iOS build number has increased.
- Confirm the user-facing version is correct.
- Archive and upload the iOS build through Xcode, Transporter, or future App
  Store automation.

App Store Connect:

- Enter App Review notes from store/app_store/app-review/review-notes.txt.
- Complete App Privacy details using store/app_store/app-privacy/privacy-notes.txt.
- Confirm screenshots meet App Store device-size requirements.
- Submit to TestFlight first.

After TestFlight:

- Verify install and login flow on a real iPhone.
- Verify demo login.
- Verify Zaptec sync.
- Verify filters, totals, PDF export, and settings.
