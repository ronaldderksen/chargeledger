Target audience

Play Console answers:

- Target age group:
  18 and over.

- Is the app designed for children?
  No.

- Does the store listing appeal to children?
  No.

- Is the app subject to Families policy requirements?
  No, based on the intended audience.

Reasoning:

Charge Ledger is an operational utility for adults who own, manage, or use
Zaptec EV chargers. It requires a Zaptec account and access to charger history.
The app is not designed for children and does not use child-directed language,
characters, games, or imagery.
