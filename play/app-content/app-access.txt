App access / Sign-in details

Play Console answers:

- Does all or part of your app require login credentials?
  Yes.

- Are all features available without special access?
  No. The main app requires a Zaptec account to show charging data.

- Access type:
  Demo account.

- Credential type:
  Account name only. No password is required for the demo account.

- Reviewer instructions:
  1. Open Charge Ledger.
  2. On the Zaptec login screen, enter `demo` as the account name.
  3. Leave the password field empty.
  4. Tap Log in.
  5. Tap Sync to load the demo charger and dummy charge sessions.
  6. Review filters, totals, charge history, PDF export, and settings.
